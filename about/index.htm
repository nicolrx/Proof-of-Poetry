Proof of Poetry

About


Proof of Poetry allows you to create randomly a poem using pieces of the Bitcoin's White Paper wrote by Satoshi Nakamoto.


My Name is Nicolas Le Roux, I'm a student and I love to build projects in my spare time. Sometimes, the Tech world lacks of art and poetry. That's why I decided to create this side project. The idea was to extract randomly sentences of the Bitcoin's White Paper (from 3 to 8 words). Then, I used a Javascript library called "double-metaphone" to be able to create a poem with ABAB rhymes.


I wanted that the user could choose an input to generate the poem. That's why I used Algolia Instant Search to allow the user to search on the fly the thousands of extracted sentences and pick one. Then, the app searches randomly the other poetry lines.
